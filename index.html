Reflect

In the OT, the design of the Tabernacle and the Temple references many elements from nature and is meant to recall the original paradise of Eden (1 Kings 6-7). At the same time, the descriptions of Eden in Genesis allude to it being a sacred space and the first humans as priestly functionaries (Gen 2:8-17).
